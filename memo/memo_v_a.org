# reduced volume and normalized area difference

parameters which determin vesicle shape

1: reduced volume ($v$)

$v = 6\sqrt \pi  \displaystyle \frac{V}{A^{3/2}}$

V...volume
A...surface area

2: area difference ($\Delta a$)

$\Delta a = \displaystyle \frac{\int HdA}{2 \sqrt \pi A^{1/2}}$

H...mean curvature
